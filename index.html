Basic Human Rights Everyone Should Know

  • Right to Equality: Every person is equal before the law.
  • Right to Freedom: Includes freedom of speech, expression, and religion.
  • Right to Life: Everyone has the right to life and personal liberty.
  • Right to Education: Every child has the right to free and compulsory education.
  • Right Against Exploitation: No one can be forced to work against their will.
  • Right to Constitutional Remedies: You can approach the court if your rights are violated.

Indian Penal Code (IPC)

  • IPC 302: Punishment for murder
  • IPC 376: Punishment for rape
  • IPC 420: Cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property
  • IPC 498A: Cruelty by husband or his relatives
  • IPC 144: Power to issue orders in urgent cases of nuisance or apprehended danger

Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC)

  • CrPC 41: Arrest without warrant
  • CrPC 125: Maintenance of wife, children, and parents
  • CrPC 154: Information in cognizable cases (FIR)
  • CrPC 167: Procedure when investigation cannot be completed in 24 hours

Motor Vehicles Act (MVA)

  • MVA 177: General provision for punishment of offences
  • MVA 185: Driving by a drunken person or by a person under the influence of drugs
  • MVA 190: Using vehicle in unsafe condition
  • MVA 194: Penalty for overloading, no helmet, etc.
